1. Choose five digits, each between 0–9. Order matters for most prize types.

2. Drawings take place twice daily: 1:30 PM and 9:45 PM ET.

4. You can play different bet types: Straight, Box, Straight/Box, Front Pair, Back Pair, or 1-OFF.

Straight (Exact Match): 1 in 100,000 — Prize: up to $50,000

Box (Any Order): Varies by number of repeating digits — 1 in 20,000 to 1 in 41,667

Straight/Box: Pays partial for any order, full for exact — splits your $1 ticket

1-OFF: Match numbers one digit higher/lower — lower odds, smaller prize

Genie’s Insight: Pick 5 offers high payouts for a low buy-in, but has extreme variance.

Strategic Tip: Because the odds are steep, it’s a “long-shot” game—best for rare plays, not daily grinding.

Recommended: Try Straight/Box or 1-OFF for a better chance at partial wins without needing exact order.
