Odds and Probability


1. Strait: Match numbers in exact order. 1$ strait play pays off $50.00 with odds of 1 : 100

2. Box: Box play odds of 1:50

3. 1-OFF: You can be 1-OFF on either 1 or both numbers for a prize of $3.00 with 8 possible winning combinations or 1:25 odds.

4. Front number OR back number: You have a 1:10 chance of drawing the 1st number or the 2nd for a $5.00 prize


What to know about odds

What is important to understand about the odds of winning a lottery game like this is the "1:100" odds. It means that if you played the game 100 times, you should expect, on average to win once. This ratio of "1:100" can also be expressed as 1/100 or 1 out of 100
