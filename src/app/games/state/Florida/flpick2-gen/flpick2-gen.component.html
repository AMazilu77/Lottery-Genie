1. Select the amount you want to play (Pick two numbers between 0-9): 50 cents or $1 (all Straight/Box and 1-OFF plays cost $1).

2. There are two drawings per day seven days per week, at approximately 1:30 p.m. and 9:45 p.m. ET. Tickets for each drawing can be purchased up to 13 minutes prior to the applicable drawing – 1:17 p.m. ET for the midday drawings and 9:32 p.m. ET for the evening drawings.

3. Select the draw time. Select MID for the midday drawing (1:30 p.m. ET), EVE for the evening drawing (9:45p.m. ET) draw, or BOTH for both the midday and evening drawings. If no draw time is marked, the terminal will automatically print a ticket for the next available draw. If BOTH is selected, two separate tickets will print – one for the midday drawing and one for the evening drawing.

You can win a top prize of $50 with a $1 Straight play, or increase your chances of winning with a Box, Straight/Box, Front/Back Number or 1-OFF play.

Reminder: Prizes must be claimed within 180 days of the draw date. All PICK 2 prizes may be claimed at any authorized Florida Lottery retailer.

The odds of winning on a straight Pick 2 ticket are 1 in 100. Boxed odds are slightly better depending on format.

This game has low variance and fast feedback. You can apply frequency tracking, trailing digit avoidance, and rolling analysis across day/night draws.
